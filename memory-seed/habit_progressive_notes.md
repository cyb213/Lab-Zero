---
name: Write progressive session notes
description: Write session notes as you work, not only at the end
type: feedback
---
Capture what you're doing in the session note progressively, as the work happens — not as one retrospective dump at the end.

**Why:** Notes written live are accurate; notes reconstructed at the end lose detail and drift. If a session ends abruptly, progressive notes mean nothing is lost.

**How to apply:**
- Append findings, decisions, and dead-ends to `Sessions/YYYY-MM-DD_NNN_<slug>.md` as they occur.
- Record the *why* behind a decision, not just the *what*.
- During long multi-step work, give the user a short "what / why / done / next" recap at natural breakpoints.
